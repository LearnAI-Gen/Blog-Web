Steering AI in Social Work

Published on May 5, 2025 | By You

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly transforming industries — including social work. The potential to support, monitor, and even predict human needs opens up many new avenues for intervention and care.

But with that comes ethical concerns: data privacy, bias in algorithms, and loss of human touch. This article discusses how we can ethically guide AI's integration into one of society’s most compassionate professions.

Balancing innovation and empathy is key. Let's explore what responsible AI in social work looks like today and what it could become tomorrow.
